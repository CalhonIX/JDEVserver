What does JDEV do besides Garry's Mod?

Currently, CalhonIX, Semtux811 and a few other developers are working on a realistic "Airsoft RP" gamemode inside of s&box, featuring realistic indoor/outdoor environments, and realistic gameplay to match. This page will get updated periodically with progress, and is under extremely heavy development.

If you are experienced with any Source 2 Tooling and are interested enough to help, contact @calhonix on discord!

faq

Common questions we recieve about the sandbox server.

What is the difference between this project and other airsoft projects?

Our version of a very common type of game is going to take full advantage of the multiplayer tools within s&box to create a much more fun and realistic experience. Our experience with airsoft always includes banter, a little bit of rule-breaking, and just having fun. So, we try to include things like proximity chat, casual gamemodes, spawn walkbacks, and field marshals instead of a classic moderation system to keep things extremely unique and attempt to capture more of what makes airsoft fun in real life.

Can I use this project's assets and code for my own?

Absolutely! We just request that you credit us somewhere in your project.

Can I run my own dedicated server?

Yes, however, we are not going to provide documentation nor instructions on setting this up. We're super amatuer developers and constantly changing things around, and eventually will make that experience more clean. However, for now, it's not a super great experience and you should definitely keep that in mind when setting up/running/maintaining your server.
