A network dedicated to providing the best possible player experience, without forcing the community to fund it.

Our Discord

Who are we?

We are just another server network, that started as a way to play together as a friend group, and we wish to expand & share that love of very community-oriented servers that we feel is sort of missing.

Why Join?

We attempt to offer the most fair, safest, and stable experience while still allowing you, the player, to play however you want. Want to sweat your ass off in classic source PVP? We love that just as much as the people who join us wanting to chill & be creative.

Features

Our game servers have a strict no-p2w design, whether thats a Minecraft server, or Garry's mod server, you can be sure your experience won't be any less fun just because you haven't paid us directly.
