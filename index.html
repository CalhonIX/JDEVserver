Server Rules/Guidelines

Welcome to our server!

The rules here are split up into specifics, for ease of reading. Staff must quote rule(s) when punishing a player so they can refer back here.

Our warning structure is 3 warns for a 3 day ban. 5 warns is a permanent ban until appeal. After a month without any warns you will be cleared.

E2/wire/pac rules:

  1. Anything that affects the server’s overall performance may lead to a ban.

  2. Anything that affects PVP (player scaling, hitboxes, offsets) will also result in an instant ban.

IF YOU ARE HAVING ERRORS OR SEE MISSING TEXTURES: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=793457825

Content Notice:

This server does use custom content made specifically for us or by us that isn’t available in our collections. Redistribution, use, and modification is strictly prohibited by other(s) than the JDEV server group.

Sandbox Rules:

  1. You may not proppush/propkill/propfly (vehicle ramming does NOT count)

  2. Attempting to gain an advantage in PVP (Large health pools, impossible hitboxes, point glitches) is an INSTANT unappealable ban. We are just trying to have building and killing unrestricted without dicks ruining it!

  3. Any action that is considered to be deliberately attempting to crash the server OR attempting to harm the overall player experience WILL result in an instant, permanent ban.

  4. Don’t overly disrupt other players’ buildings or be an overall annoying fuck.

  5. Blocked off areas MUST be accompanied by a working keypad/fading door entrance. This allows for you to build bases and propblock areas of the map, ONLY if you have a valid keypad entrance that can be cracked.

  6. Overpowered death machines/mechs/tanks are not allowed. We don’t wanna ban these cool creations so please keep them within reason and not unfair!

  7. Any unfair advantages/hacks/exploits are not permitted

  8. Player reports MUST contain substantial evidence OR contain the time of which the violation(s) occured

  9. Don’t attack grounded WAC UNLESS its near spawn.

Chat Rules:

  1. Banter and general discourse is allowed and encouraged. However, slurs/racist/derogatory comments are strictly off-limits. (i.e. you can say “ur dogshit kid” but not “ur dogshit f*g”)

    1. Despite the rule above, try and keep everything friendly and fun instead of a toxic cesspool.

  2. Staff have immediate discretion, if you disagree or think a decision is incorrect, you’d get a lot more accomplished by contacting our discord instead of arguing.

  3. Micspam/Chatspam are not allowed, you will permanently lose chat/mic function for being annoying.

  4. If you use a mic, you must be 13+ or you will be gagged.

  5. If you are having a heated gamer moment and being overly annoying/disrespectful or take banter too far, instead of being warned you may be given a “cool off” mute, this is your chance to chill out and avoid a ban.

  6. Crying for staff without a good reason or forcing staff online without a good reason will more than likely result in being ticket blacklisted.
