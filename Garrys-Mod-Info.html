So what is JDEV PVP Sandbox?

The JDEV Garry’s Mod server is dedicated to a deathmatch-like experience while still allowing buildings to flourish. This encourages active PvP, along with base wars and building. You get points for each kill, allowing you to buy restricted weapons, case keys, cosmetics, and even ranks/donor vouchers. However, our server doesn’t force you to fight to the death, so you can still join and build without being bothered in the provided PvP free building zones.

This page is dedicated to every little piece of info you could ever want to know about our Garry's mod server.

Before continuing, if you haven't, you should read our server rules

Map Guide
The default map the server uses is  gm_genesis , and the spawn room is completely protected. If you don't want to cross through any PVP areas, you can use !warp zone1,zone2, etc.
gm_bigcity_improved has building zones covering plots of grassy area.
gm_7eleven_v6 has a building zone underground
gm_northwest_airfield  has protected hangars, !warp zone1
There are more zones not listed, and the server will notify you if you enter them.

faq

Common questions we recieve about the sandbox server.

What if I don't want to PVP?

The server features zones which you can enter using the methods listed above. Other than that, build a really good base.

How can I become staff?

We run staff applications periodically on our Discord.

The requirements are at least 1 day on the server, being 16+ and speaking fluent english or spanish.

What does the server's rank system look like?

User Ranks - Earnable Through Playtime
Recruit - Default Rank
Member - 2h of playtime or through !discord
Trusted - 24h of playtime for discord, 72h for non-discord members.
Decorated - 168h of playtime - NOTE: decorated and legendary are meant for people who grind it, after trusted the pointshop ranks are the intended progression.
Legendary - 336h of playtime

Pointshop Ranks - Purchasable with points if you've joined our discord. NOTE: PS ranks are equivalent to -2 tiers of their donor. A PS T3 rank = Dono T1 rank. The only difference between ranks in the same tier is tag, and the benefits will be listed on the first rank of the tier.
Tier 1 - 500 Kills
Killer -  Same rank as decorated, without the playtime.
Tier 2 - 750 Kills
Rising Star -  Same as Legendary, included discord rank, increased point drops, and an increased prop limit.
Veteran -  Available to members of the server prior to June 1st, 2023 only. 
Tier 3 - 1250 Kills
Shining star -  Equiv to Tier 1 Donor Rank
Tier 4 - Dependant
Shooting Star -  In between T1 and T2 Donor rank. Features unrestricted access to select knives, and a higher vehicle limit than donor ranks.
Champion -  Always available to winners of events, and as the rarest drop in the server.
Star Champion - Available if you manage to win an event as a Champion.

Donor Ranks - Available to those who support the server  NOTE: All payments to the server are FINAL, and we will never provide p2w benefits to donor ranks.
Tier 1 - 2.50$ USD
Donor -  Access to donor zones (ex. gm_bigcity hidden room), !setwarp, donor pointshop, and exclusive access to donor-only discord channels (Not shared with PS ranks). You also recieve 250 Premium Points, which can only be spent on cosmetics.
Tier 2 - 5.00$ USD
Elite -   !goto !bring !tp,  Access to regular duplicator tool, as well as all previous rewards. You also recieve 500 Premium Points.
Tier 3 - 10.00$ USD
Elite+  - Includes an animated tag, permanent boost to all sandbox limits, access to cosmetic black market (cheap skinned weapons) and an increased chance of finding crates. You also recieve 750 Premium Points.
Tier 4 - 15.00$ USD
Paragon -  !noclip, !giveammo, !strip, !ignite, A more powerful vote in votemaps, Access to beta servers and testing content, and a much fancier animated tag. You also recieve 750 Premium Points.
Tier 5 - 20.00$ USD
Divine -  You get a rainbow tag, custom !loadout, and a sincere thanks for supporting an entire month of core operating costs with one donation! You also recieve 1000 Premium Points.
Divine - >25.00$ USD
Any donation past 25 dollars allows for you to get a completely custom tag, this means if you just feel generous and want to support the server, you'll recieve the highest rank + a custom tag. If any donation tiers come out that are less than what you paid for a custom tag, you'll recieve that rank instead, of course keeping your tag.

NOTE: All donations are one-time and final, paypal links and crypto addresses are in the #ranks-info channel on discord. Unlike the legacy server, your payments will be honoured network-wide and forever, including future servers under the JDEV branding.
Ranks are not automatically added, please contact calhonix on discord after/before your donation, and you'll recieve your package. You can also contact calhonix for questions, concerns, or comments.

What will happen once S&Box releases?

Well it just so happens that I, the lead developer of JDEV, am by trade a C# programmer, and not actually all that fluent in Lua. I got a pre-release key and have been cooking up some stuff that the JDEV playerbase is sure to enjoy. We cannot promise that we remake the JDEV server in s&box, but we can promise that we will release something that the community will love for it!

The Garry's Mod server will also more than likely stay running, as no one really envisions the game going anywhere anytime soon.

What happened to the original JDEV servers?

Honestly, the original 8 player slot JDEV server was a weird little project I developed after me and my friends got banned from another sandbox server we played on. This time, we're coming back and using everything we learned from trying to expand that server and creating a much bigger project, and building the community to stay.
