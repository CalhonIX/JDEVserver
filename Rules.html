Server Rules

These apply while you are on our server, whether you read them or not.
Don't want to PVP? Our server features safezones, ask staff if you cant find any & don't want to fight!

Game Rules

  1. No prop abuse (killing, flying, pushing)
  2. Try to get along with one another, the server is most fun when everyone is having a good time.
  3. No intentional harming of the server's performance, nor threatening to. (ex. server crashing)
  4. No blocking off any area of the map without a working keypadded entrance.
  5. No hacks/scripts/exploits.
  6. No killing while in any state of invulnerability. (ex. sitting on some props & shooting)
  7. No abusing safe zones (ex. PVP bases, cannons, and vehicle camping inside of build zones)
  8. Do not disrupt other player's activities in a malicious manner. (ex. destroying things inside of a build zone)
  9. Don't kill farm for points, or abuse bugs that give points via unintended methods.
  10. This server is intended for 13+ audiences, being unquestionably under 13 will result in a ban.

Chat Rules & Less Strictly Enforced Rules.

  1. No hateful speech (slurs, derogatory terms, overly profane insults)
  2. Disagreements with staff decisions should not be handled by screaming in chat, we have a !discord.
  3. No spamming of either voice or text chat.
  4. It's a PVP server, we get banter and tilting, but keep it reasonable or you may be temporarily muted to chill out.
  5. No advertising other servers.
  6. Use common sense.
  7. No soundboards.
  8. Try to avoid killing players directly with explosives. (excluding bombing, grenades, and drones)
  9. Don't attack grounded/unarmed aircraft.
  10. As long as what you're doing doesn't harm the experience of someone else, it's probably okay.

Helpful Info Panel

  • We make use of a lot of workshop content, you can find a collection of it here: JDEV Addon Collection
  • This server is a "Build 2 Kill" server, meaning most players are building bases or vehicles to fight with. If you don't want to PVP, you can always type "!warp build" to teleport to the map's protected building zone. Seriously complaining about being killed makes you look dumb!
  • Save your buildings & props from being deleted with Advanced Duplicator!
  • We hate pay-to-win servers, so instead most ranks are bought through the points you earn from kills, killstreaks, events, drops, and playtime.

You should join JDEV's !discord, you'll get a rank, and access to in-game events! Our discord link

Here's a list of useful in-game commands:
@<message> - Send a message to any online staff
!discord - Get a link to our discord
!ws or !workshop - Link to our workshop
!motd - Come back to this page
!warp build - warp to the current map's building zone.
