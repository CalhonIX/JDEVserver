Server Rules

These apply only to our Garry's Mod servers, s&soft is bound to a different set of rules, relayed in game.

Sandbox Rules

These rules apply in-game, and are ultimately up to staff discretion

  1. No prop abuse (killing, flying, pushing)
  2. You may not purposefully harm the performance of the server, or threaten to.
  3. Don't be annoying with buildings.
  4. Blocked areas of the map must have a functional keypad/fading door entrance.
  5. No hacks/scripts/exploits 
  6. Refrain from attacking grounded aircraft, or unarmed aircraft.
  7. Turrets/mechs/custom vehicles are not allowed if they make you invulnerable or are insta-kills.
  8. No gaining points via unintended methods (i.e NPC spam killing)
  9. Although not strictly enforced, try to refrain from killing players directly with explosives.

Chat Rules

Our chat relays to our discord server, it's logged and available for anyone to see.

  1. No hateful speech (slurs, derogatory terms, overly profane insults)
  2. Staff have immediate discretion, if you disagree with a decision, use our discord.
  3. No micspam/chat spam. If you have a mic, be 13+.
  4. Being too heated/toxic in chat will get you temporarily muted until you can calm down
  5. No advertising other servers.
  6. Use common sense, be friendly, and don't make playing a game with you a hassle.
  7. Soundboards are only allowed if you manage to not annoy anyone with it.

Server Specific Info/Warnings

Special info for the server & helpful tips for new players.

  • Hitbox/Health manipulation is a very quick ban, do not change your hitbox in PAC3 and do not abuse items to gain giant health pools
  • You cannot complain about PVP, like ever, if you can't find a build zone or a warp isn't working, talk to staff.
  • We employ a point based rewards system, kills, killstreaks, and vehicle frags will all contribute to your points, which you can access in !shop.
  • Off duty staff are still staff, listen to them, and you can tell if a staff member is off duty or not by using TAB or by looking at their tag when they chat.

You are encouraged to join our discord, it makes playing here a lot more fun & you get free stuff! Our discord link

This server uses a workshop collection to manage addons. You can find that here or with !workshop in-game

Contact @calhonix on discord for questions/concerns.
