Whether you join our Sandbox server, or run around in our Airsoft-based game, you'll have an excellent, fair, and fun experience.

Our Discord

Who are we?

JDEV started as a small group of friends searching for a way to play together as a friend group, however, we ended up wishing to expand and share our love of community oriented sandbox servers that we feel is missing from the modern day Garry’s Mod experience.

Why Join?

Our sandbox server offers a fair, safe, and stable experience to the player, while still allowing our user base to play however they please. Want to sweat your ass off in classic source PVP? Go ahead, we love that just as much as the people who join us wanting to chill, be creative, and show off their building skills.

What do you offer?

We offer a Garry's Mod server, and soon we will release a s&box gamemode revolving around airsoft. Every single one of our projects are strictly non p2w, and we will never introduce purchasable perks that affect gameplay in any way.
